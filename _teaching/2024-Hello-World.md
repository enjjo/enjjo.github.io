This course explores the role of traditional writing skills in the digital age, focusing on tools like Grammarly and ChatGPT. It investigates the interplay between writing and technology, examining platforms like GitHub and LaTeX and addressing issues such as technology and information gaps. Through readings and practical exercises, students use digital tools like Zotero, Google Docs, and Miro, and learn data visualization with Tableau. The course emphasizes developing writing skills, critical reading abilities, and understanding the transformative impact of digital tools on academic writing.
